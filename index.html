How To Play

Do not guess the word in six tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

  • Each correctly guessed letter in the correct spot will deduct one point from your score.

  • Each guess must contain at least one unused letter from previous guesses.
